Frequently Asked Questions

What is Noundry?

Noundry is a modern .NET development platform that provides intelligent tools, reusable components, and AI-powered assistance to accelerate your journey from idea to production.

Which .NET versions are supported?

Noundry supports .NET 6.0, .NET 8.0, and .NET 9.0. All our libraries are multi-targeted for maximum compatibility.

Can I use Noundry for commercial projects?

Yes! With a Developer, Team, or Enterprise license, you can use Noundry for commercial projects.

How do I get support?

Community support is available via Discord and Stack Overflow. Team and Enterprise plans include priority support with dedicated response times.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. Try Noundry risk-free.
